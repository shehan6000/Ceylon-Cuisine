Pol Sambol

An incredible easy Sri Lankan coconut Sambal recipe that only takes 10 mins to make and need very few ingredients. Goes well with every meal.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dishes

instructions

step 1

Add chili flakes, chili powder, and salt to the mortar and pestle. Pound it to make a coarse powder. Salt makes it easier to grind.

step 2

Wash the mortar and pestle. Then, add the grated coconut and ground dried red chili flakes. Bash and muddle them using a pestle until they combine well.

step 3

Now, take all the mixture off to a plate or bowl. Then, add the lime juice and mix everything well.

ingredients

1.5 cup Fresh shredded coconut/ grated coconut

1 shallot chopped or 4 Asian shallots or 2 tbsp chopped onion

2 teaspoons chili flakes

1/2 teaspoon chili powder

1/2 teaspoons salt (adjust to your taste)s

2 teaspoons lime juice or small piece of lime (adjust to your taste)

1 habanero (optional)

tools

Mortar and pestle

Knife
