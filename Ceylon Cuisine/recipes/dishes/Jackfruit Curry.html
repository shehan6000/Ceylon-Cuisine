Jackfruit Curry

Jackfruit curry is also known as kirikos. Made with creamy coconut milk, this popular curry is a must-try traditional jackfruit recipe.

prep time

1hr

cook time

30 min

serving

4 servings

Tags : Dishes

instructions

step 1

Clean and deseed the Jackfruit.

step 2

Cut the Jackfruit into thin long stripes.

step 3

Clean the seeds and cut them into two.

step 4

Mix the Jackfruit, the seeds and all the other ingredients except the mustard and the coconut milk in a pan.

step 5

Add a cup of water to it and stir. Put the pan on a medium flame cover and cook until the jackfruit is well done.

step 6

Grind the mustard seed and stir it to the Coconut milk.

step 7

Add this to the pan and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.

step 8

Serve hot

ingredients

3 cups of Jackfruit

1 medium onion, finely chopped

2 green chilies

1 tsp Maldivian fish(optional)

2 cups coconut milk

1 tsp mustard seed

4-5 curry leaves

1/2 tsp turmeric powder

1 tsp curry powder

1 tsp chili powder

salt to taste.

tools

Large Pot/ cooker

Knife
