Milk Rice (Kiribath)

In Sri Lanka, milk rice / Kiribath can be considered the staple food on special occasions. Milk rice / Kiribath is widely used as a breakfast food. This delicious dish is made using rice and coconut milk mainly. Milk rice is often eaten with lunu miris (Katta Sambol).

prep time

15 min

cook time

25 min

serving

6-8servings

Tags : Dishes

instructions

step 1

Rinse the rice well.

step 2

Put the rice and water into a saucepan and cook on medium flame.

step 3

Stir the rice frequently.

step 4

Add table salt to the thick coconut milk and mix.

step 5

When the rice is cooked correctly add coconut milk and stir it well on medium/low flame.

step 6

When the milk rice comes to the dough, transfer it to a flat tray or banana leaf.

step 7

Spread the milk rice on the flat tray or banana leaf evenly.

step 8

Leave it to cool for about 30 minutes.

step 9

Cut into the desired shape and serve.

ingredients

250 g Raw white rice

2 cups thick coconut milk

1¼ tsp Table salt

3½ cups Water

tools

Large Pot/ cooker

Tray
