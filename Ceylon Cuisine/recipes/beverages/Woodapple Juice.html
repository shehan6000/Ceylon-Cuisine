Woodapple Juice

Wood apple fruit is rich in antioxidants and also contains several compounds that fight against free radicals and help in better functioning of the body. The pulp of the fruit also acts as a natural detoxifier and helps in protecting the Liver and Kidney health

prep time

6 min.

cook time

1 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : Beverages

instructions

step 1

Break the wood apple with the help of a rolling pin or any hard object.

step 2

With the help of a spoon scoop out the soft pulp. Once scooped out, put it in a bowl add 1 cup water. Let it rest for 5 mins. 

step 3

Next, mash the pulp very well and using a strainer, strain the mashed mixture in parts. 

step 4

Keep adding water as required while straining. Once strained, add jaggery, salt and milk and stir well. 

step 5

In a glass, add some ice cubes and mint leaves and pour the pulp. Add a little more water to adjust the consistency and serve chilled. top.

ingredients

3 woodapples of medium size

3-3.5 cups of chilled water

12 tablespoons of jaggery or as required

2 pinches of salt

tools

None
