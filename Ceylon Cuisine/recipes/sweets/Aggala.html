Aggala

Aggala is one of the most delicious sweets in Sri Lanka. This is also a sweet item that can be found in the Aurudu table. This sweet is prepared with roasted rice, jaggery or honey, coconuts, and pepper and has a mild sweetness and peppery flavour.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Sweets

instructions

step 1

Take a pot and add a small amount of sugar. Then heat it until brown color. Next add the rest of sugar and water keeping stirring. Then let to dissolve the sugar.

step 2

After it becomes as the texture of honey, remove from the heat. Then place it aside.

step 3

Take another pot and add coconut. Then cook them for a few minutes.

step 4

Next add the sugar syrup to it. Mix well and remove from the heat.

step 5

Now add roasted rice flour little by little to the coconut mixture and mix well. If the mixture is dry, you can add a little hot water.

step 6

After that add salt and pepper to it. Now, ready the mixture.

step 7

Then make balls from the mixure and coat them with roasted rice flour. It is served as a snack and with a cup of tea.

ingredients

1 cup of roasted rice flour

1/4 cup of coconut

100g of sugar

125ml of water

1/2 tsp of pepper

tools

2 pots
