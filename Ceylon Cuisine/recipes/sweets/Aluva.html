Aluwa

Aluwa is another Sri Lankan sweet that is enjoyed during avurudu and Sri Lankan & Tamil new year.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

30 servings

Tags : Sweets

instructions

step 1

Roast the rice flour under medium heat. It is better to grind the rice yourself. If you use the processed rice flour aluwa will get sticky and have a different taste. Keep little amount of roasted flour for dusting and coating..

step 2

Boil the sugar and water in non-stick pan until form a thick line and take out from the heat.

step 3

While continuously stirring add ground cardamom seeds and chopped cashew nuts. This time will allow the syrup to little cool and help to keep aluwa in good white colour.

step 4

Then add the roasted flour and mix in well.

step 5

Quickly spread on a tray lined with waxed paper and try to keep it at 1 cm thickness.

step 6

Cut in to pieces promptly. After few minutes separate from the tray and coat with remaining flour.

ingredients

10 cups of rice flour

2 cups of sugar

3 cups of water

3-4 cardomoms

Handful of cashew nuts

tools

A wok

A tray
